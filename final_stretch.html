It's the Final Stretch!

20-JAN-2013

Hooray! Comcast has provided the costs for the Phase II build-out, and we're ready to start fundraising! You'll find all the details on the Contribute Now! page, but the work the Mountain Broadband team did over the summer has lowered costs by 50% from what was announced in June!

The second big change is that our deadline is now February 28th! The change is because Comcast is eager to get started with the planning, and therefore would like to get our portion of the money sooner so they can get started sooner.

The time has come for each of us to decide what having reliable, wired, high-speed internet access is worth to us. Recall all the benefits, and consider the cost savings as you decide how much you would like to contribute to your route's fundraiser.
