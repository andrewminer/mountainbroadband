Neighborhood Routes

Comcast will consider each route individually for whether they will be included in phase 2. Each route will be responsible for raising the money needed to cover their portion of the costs. Any route which cannot raise sufficient money will not receive service.

All of the route leaders listed below are volunteers who live along the routes mentioned. They all attend our regular meetings, and are very knowledgable about the project. If you notice something incorrect on the map, you have any questions, or you just have a comment or suggestion for us, please contact them directly.

The maps are those we created and sent to Comcast as our suggested routes. At this point, they are suggestions only, and Comcast has not yet approved them. We will update the website with final routes when we hear back from Comcast. The map's legend shows the meaning of the various symbols.

Please find your home on the following maps and contact your team leader.
