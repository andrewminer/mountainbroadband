Welcome to Mountain Broadband

10-SEP-2012

This website provides information for anyone interested in the ongoing efforts to bring high-speed internet access to the Loma Prieta community in the Santa Cruz Mountains. This has been a multi-year, multi-phase project. Phase I of the project is nearly complete with service expected to come online in the next few weeks. Phase II is finishing the planning stage and is about to enter the fundraising stage.

Read the About section for more project history

The Phase II build out will be organized according to individual neighborhoods corresponding to the routes the cable will follow. Each neighborhood will need to pay for a portion of the cost for building out that route. Those routes that can raise the necessary funds will be included in the build out, while those that cannot, will not. To that end, we will be organizing a fundraising effort in each neighborhood in the next few weeks.

Examine the Routes and contact your route leader

It costs about $60,000 per mile to install the necessary cable and hardware for wired internet service and we have over 13 miles to cover, so this is going to be a huge project. It is also a huge investment with a number of far-reaching benefits.

Read about the Benefits of having wired, high-speed internet

Unfortunately, we won't have exact answers to many relevant questions of cost, schedule, service levels, etc. until we hear back from Comcast about our proposal. In the meantime however, we are here to share what information we do have, and get answers to your questions.

Read the F.A.Q. for more information

We're your neighbors, friends and family, and we all want wired, high-speed connections in our homes too. We're volunteering our time to try to make the most of this opportunity to get a faster, cheaper and more reliable connection. While Comcast is currently the best contender, we're not affiliated with them in any way. We just want the best service we can get for the best price. We hope we can count on your help.

Mountain Broadband is a group of volunteers dedicated to bringing wired broadband coverage to the Loma Prieta region of the Santa Cruz mountains.
While the current best option for broadband appears to be Comcast, Mountain Broadband is not affiliated with Comcast, nor do we represent Comcast as the only solution.
