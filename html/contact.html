Contact Information

Your first point of contact should be your route leader. They attend our regular meetings and have all the information available. They are also your neighbors and have the same interests you do! Please click the map to the right to find your home and determine your route leader.

If you have a correction or question about this website, or are unsure who your route leader is, you can contact Andrew Miner or Larry Lopp.

Mountain Broadband is a group of volunteers dedicated to bringing wired broadband coverage to the Loma Prieta region of the Santa Cruz mountains.
While the current best option for broadband appears to be Comcast, Mountain Broadband is not affiliated with Comcast, nor do we represent Comcast as the only solution.
