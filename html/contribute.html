Contribute Now!

In order to participate in the Phase II build-out, each route will independently need to raise the funds necessary to match Comcast's funds. If a route manages to raise the necessary funds, then they will be included in the build-out which will begin during the summer of 2013.

Since each route has different distances to cover, different terrain, and different numbers of homes participating, each route has different costs. In order to help people decide how much to contribute, we've recommended Gold, Silver, and Bronze contribution levels for each route. This is only a recommendation based upon the costs of the project, and if you find that you wish to contribute more or less than these amounts, any and all assistance is most welcome!

How to Contribute

1. Find your route on this page, and decide whether the gold, silver, or bronze level (or some other amount) best matches your interest in the project.

2. Write a check to the Santa Cruz Mountain Alliance for the amount you've chosen. Be sure to include "Mountain Broadband" and your route letter in the memo field!


If your check doesn't have the address to be serviced, please include that as well.

3. Deliver your check to your route leader (listed below).

If you have any questions, check out the F.A.Q. page!
